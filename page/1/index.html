What your Restaurant Needs

Mobile Focused

Its 2020 if your website does not look good on a phone. Your business is losing money as we speak with not establishing a good first impression.

Online ordering

No matter where your customer wants to eat. You have to be able to get that food to them easily and efficiently. Whether its Uber eats or ideally having your own online ordering on your website.

Easy to view the menu

Lets face it people like to see what kind of food you offer before they step in the door. A hard to read PDF won't work any more. Its go to be easy to read and clickable to see what ingredients you use in your meal.

Email collection

It doesn't need to be aggressive but collecting emails is a major key in getting returning clients. You may get them in the door once but offering specials and promotions is how you get them to come again.
